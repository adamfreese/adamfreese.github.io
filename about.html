This is the personal website of me, Adam Freese. I'm a theoretical nuclear physicist specializing in hadron structure. I decided to make a personal website on a whim. It'll just be an assortment of random things that I find interesting.

The background photo is from quad at the University of Washington in Seattle. This quad is where my wife and I reunited after several years of being apart, and the cherry blossoms were in full bloom when we met there. The location and cherry blossoms are both special to me, which is why I chose it as a background image. I also prominently use the colors green and purple (especially for links), which are my and her favorite colors respectively.

In general, I plan to add things to this website casually and haphazardly; it's not a systematic project. However, I've lately been doing a lot of group theory, so I'll most likely add some group theory pages in the near future. This gives me a good excuse to brush up on the math and make sure I get sign conventions right.

Since the website is under a .org domain, I plan to keep all the content here educational and free. Nothing will be sold or advertised here.
